# CameraTrap_MammalDiversity
Waterhole visitation rates studied in wet and dry season in the Kruger National Park, South Africa, using camera traps. Results are published in: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0006320725004070.
Dataset at: 
Marjolein EM Toorinas, & Maxwell J Farrell. (2024). Kruger National Park from South Africa (2015 and 2022). Knowledge Network for Biocomplexity. urn:uuid:aa2e3a76-2a32-4acd-9e05-a8ac50fda40e (https://knb.ecoinformatics.org/view/urn%3Auuid%3Aaa2e3a76-2a32-4acd-9e05-a8ac50fda40e).
